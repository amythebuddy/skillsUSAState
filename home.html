About Our Foundation

We're all about making Chino Valley a better place by focusing on stuff like helping businesses grow, making sure people have good jobs, keeping our environment clean, and making sure our community is a fun place to live. Our new website needs to share all about this important work in a way that gets people excited and wanting to help out.
